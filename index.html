Daeyoung Kim

Research Engineer

I am a Research Engineer at KT (Korea Telecom), where I am currently developing an omni-modality model. I received my M.S. from KAIST Graduate School of AI under the supervision of Prof. Edward Choi. I completed a B.S. in Computer Science and Information Security Convergence from Korea University.

My research interests lie in building reliable and robust machine learning models, with a strong focus on Multimodality. I am passionate about developing models that can understand and process information from multiple sources, such as images, text, and speech, while ensuring their reliability and trustworthiness.
